TEssay Analytics Customer Code of Conduct

Hello! Thank you for choosing TEssay study abroad application service. Please read the following terms carefully to ensure a smooth collaboration.

A successful partnership is key to your application success. The process can be lengthy and complex, but by working together effectively, we can reduce your stress and efficiently resolve issues. As partners, we share one goal: to help you gain admission to your dream school. We look forward to a productive collaboration.

Communication Policy

  • All official communication must be conducted via email.
  • No WeChat/WhatsApp/SMS messages will be accepted for service-related discussions.
  • We respond to application-related emails before 10:00 AM Beijing Time on business days.
  • If the customer is temporarily unavailable (e.g., due to exams, internships), notify us promptly to adjust timelines.

Professional Conduct

  • No disrespectful behavior (including insults, threats, or aggressive language) toward staff or other customers.
  • No price shaming or unsolicited comments about our pricing, business model, or operations.
  • No false or misleading information—all documents and details provided must be accurate and truthful.
  • Requests must align with your purchased service tier. Additional services require separate payment.
  • Report difficulties early—we’ll guide you through challenges.

Service Termination Policy

Violation of this code (including disrespect, harassment, or provision of fake documents) will result in:

  • Immediate termination of services.
  • Blacklisting from future services.

Payment & Refund Policy

  • Fees are non-negotiable once services commence.
  • Refunds (if applicable) follow the terms outlined in your service agreement.

Confidentiality & Responsibility

  • We protect your data, but you are responsible for providing correct information.
  • Misrepresentation (e.g., fake transcripts, fraudulent recommendations) voids all agreements.

Dispute Resolution

  • Concerns must be submitted via email for formal review.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone violating this code.

By engaging with our services, you agree to abide by these terms.


Thank you for your trust! We’re committed to providing high-quality, efficient service and wish you success in your applications.
